---
title: Stablecoin
slug: stablecoin
short: A cryptocurrency designed to track the value of a reference asset, typically a fiat currency like the US dollar, so balances stay predictable rather than swinging with the crypto market.
category: tokens
level: beginner
link: /stablecoins
mentalModel: "A digital IOU pegged to a real-world currency. Some stablecoins hold actual dollars in a bank to back the peg (fiat-backed), others lock crypto collateral and use code to keep the price in line (algorithmic). The peg only holds as long as the backing mechanism does."
related: [djed, usdm]
---

On Cardano, USDM is a fiat-backed stablecoin and Djed is an algorithmic one; the two illustrate the main families of stablecoin design currently in use.
