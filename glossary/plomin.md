The Conway-era hard fork that activated the remaining CIP-1694 governance actions on January 29, 2025. Where Chang enabled only parameter changes and hard-fork initiations, Plomin opened up the rest: treasury withdrawals, constitution updates, motions of no confidence, committee changes, and new constitutional committee members.
