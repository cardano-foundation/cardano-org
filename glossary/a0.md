A network parameter that scales how strongly operator pledge influences reward distribution. A higher `a0` means pools with larger pledges earn proportionally more rewards than equally sized pools with low pledge; a lower `a0` flattens the difference and weights pools more by their total delegation.

`a0` works together with `k` to shape stake pool incentives toward both decentralization and operator commitment.
