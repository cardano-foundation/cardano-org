---
title: DRep
slug: drep
short: A delegate elected by ada holders to vote on Cardano governance actions on their behalf; the on-chain equivalent of electing a representative in parliament.
category: governance
link: /governance/delegate
aliases: [Delegated Representative]
mentalModel: "A voter you hand your governance power to: they study proposals so you don't have to, and you can switch your delegation at any time."
related: [cip-1694, governance-action, constitutional-committee]
---

Any ada holder can register as a DRep, and any ada holder can delegate their voting power to one. Delegation is revocable at any time and stays in effect across future governance actions until changed.
