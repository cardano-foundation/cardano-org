---
title: Bridge
slug: bridge
short: A protocol that connects Cardano to another blockchain so assets or messages can move between them; typically a pair of contracts that lock value on one chain and mint a representation on the other.
category: network
level: beginner
mentalModel: "A bridge does not literally move a token between chains. It locks the original on one side and mints a stand-in on the other. The stand-in is only worth what the lock-up still holds, which is why bridge security is mostly about the safety of that lock."
related: [stablecoin, sidechains]
---
