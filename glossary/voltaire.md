The [fifth roadmap phase of Cardano development](/research/#voltaire), focused on putting governance and treasury management into the hands of the community. Voltaire is the roadmap label; its on-chain expression is the Conway ledger era, brought live by the Chang hard fork in September 2024. Named after the French Enlightenment philosopher.
