---
title: Daedalus
slug: daedalus
short: A full-node Cardano wallet that downloads and verifies the entire blockchain on the user's device.
category: wallets
related: [wallet, yoroi, icarus]
---

A desktop wallet for Cardano that runs its own full node, downloading and validating every block locally. Daedalus does not rely on any third-party server to read the chain, which makes it heavier to set up than light wallets but removes the trust requirement on a remote backend.
