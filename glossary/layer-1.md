---
title: Layer 1
slug: layer-1
short: The base blockchain protocol itself, where transactions are finalized and the consensus rules are enforced; Cardano is a Layer 1.
category: network
level: beginner
mentalModel: "The ground floor of a blockchain stack. The rules and finality live here; everything else (Layer 2 networks, sidechains, off-chain protocols) builds on top without changing them."
related: [layer-2]
---

Layer 2 protocols (such as Hydra) build on top of a Layer 1 to add throughput or specialized behavior without changing the base chain's rules.
