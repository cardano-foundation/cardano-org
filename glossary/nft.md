---
title: NFT
slug: nft
short: A token that represents ownership of a specific unique item, recorded on-chain; anyone can copy the picture, but only one wallet holds the original record.
category: tokens
level: beginner
aliases: [Non-Fungible Token]
mentalModel: "A digital certificate of authenticity. The artwork can be copied; the certificate cannot."
related: [native-token, policy-id]
---

On Cardano, NFTs are native tokens minted with a quantity of one under a policy that cannot mint more. Because they are native, they move with the same transaction model as ada and need no smart contract to transfer.
