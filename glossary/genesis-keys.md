The seven cryptographic keys minted at the launch of Cardano and distributed across the three founding entities: three to Input Output, two to the Cardano Foundation, and two to EMURGO. During the federated Byron era these keys signed every block; as the `d` protocol parameter declined through Shelley, block production shifted to community stake pools.

`d` reached 0 on March 31, 2021, leaving block production fully in community hands. The Genesis Keys' last residual role was retired when the Vasil hard fork removed the `d` parameter entirely.
