A small set of cryptographic keys minted at the launch of Cardano and distributed to the three founding entities. During the early federated phase of the network, these keys jointly signed protocol updates so the chain could evolve while consensus among community-run stake pools was still being established.

As decentralization phases progressed and Shelley brought distributed consensus, the role of the Genesis Keys was retired.
