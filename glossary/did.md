---
title: DID
slug: did
short: An identifier you create and control yourself, with no central registrar; the record that resolves it lives on a blockchain or other decentralized system rather than in a single company's database.
category: general
aliases: [Decentralized Identifier]
mentalModel: "Like a username that no platform can revoke. The identifier is yours, the proofs that back it are yours, and the network only acts as a public ledger of who is allowed to update them."
---

DIDs are designed to let individuals carry their identity between services without depending on a specific provider to vouch for them.
