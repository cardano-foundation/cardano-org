---
title: Sidechains
slug: sidechains
short: Independent blockchains wired to Cardano through a bridge, so projects can experiment with their own rules (different VM, faster finality, custom tokenomics) while settling back to Cardano when they need its security.
category: network
mentalModel: "Spin-off ledgers running alongside the main chain. They keep their own state and their own consensus, then use a bridge to move assets back and forth when results need to land on Cardano."
related: [bridge, layer-2]
---

Sidechains differ from Layer 2 protocols: a sidechain runs its own consensus and validators, whereas a Layer 2 (like Hydra) inherits the main chain's security and only uses the main chain for settlement.
