Intersect is facilitating the election process for Cardano’s Constitutional Committee to fulfill a critical "unowned function" required by Section 3.2 of the Cardano Constitution. Because a fair election requires a structurally neutral administrator, Intersect has stepped in as a non-partisan steward rather than a participant, leveraging its established institutional knowledge in managing member-driven governance. This independent, non-commercial coordination ensures that the committee can be updated efficiently without stalling Cardano's overall governance framework.
