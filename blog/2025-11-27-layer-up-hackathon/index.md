The November 27, 2025, blog post recaps the "Layer Up" hackathon, which attracted 134 developers across 92 teams during the Cardano Summit. The event featured two tracks: an open innovation track by the Cardano Foundation and an AI-focused track by Masumi. Winners included BioFlow (health data) and KarbonSense (environmental intelligence). The initiative aims to drive blockchain adoption through practical solutions, with upcoming regional hackathons planned for Asia and Africa.
