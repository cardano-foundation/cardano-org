The "Layer Up" hackathon involved 134 developers across 92 teams during the Cardano Summit. The event featured an open innovation track and an AI-focused track by Masumi. Winners included BioFlow for health data and KarbonSense for environmental intelligence. The initiative aims to drive blockchain adoption through practical solutions, with future regional hackathons planned for Asia and Africa.
