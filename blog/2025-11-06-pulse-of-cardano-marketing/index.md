In a blog post from November 6, Intersect's Growth and Marketing Committee shared key findings from their first community-wide marketing survey. The results highlight a strong demand for unified messaging and increased support for developers and founders. Respondents prioritized DeFi and real-world use cases, advocating for treasury-backed liquidity programs. While grassroots efforts are effective, there is a clear call for better storytelling tools and a cohesive long-term vision to guide Cardano's growth.
