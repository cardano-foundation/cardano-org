The November 28, 2025, development report highlights the launch of the Leios development tracker by IOG, offering a consolidated view of the protocol's global progress. The networking team focused on Cardano node v.10.6, resolving `cardano-tracer` issues and preparing for P2P-only networking. Project Catalyst Fund15 proposal submissions closed on November 27, with the review period starting December 2. In governance, Intersect's 24-hour annual members' meeting is set to begin on November 29 in the Virtual Hub.
