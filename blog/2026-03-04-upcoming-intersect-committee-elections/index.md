Applications for the 2026 Intersect committee elections officially open March 30, inviting paid members to help steer technical and budgetary oversight. Candidates have until April 17 to step up. This year introduces a 500 ada monthly service reward and sharper transparency for voters. It’s a prime opportunity to bring diverse global voices into the room where Cardano’s substantive governance decisions are made.
