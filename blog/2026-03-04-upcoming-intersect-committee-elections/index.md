The March 04, 2026, Intersect report announces that applications for the 2026 committee elections will open on March 30 for all paid individual members. Candidates have until April 17 to apply for roles that shape technical decisions and budget oversight. New improvements include a 500 ada monthly service reward and increased transparency for voters. These elections aim to integrate diverse global voices into the substantive governance of Cardano.
