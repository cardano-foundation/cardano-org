The Cardano Foundation, EMURGO, and Rare Network shared results from their year-long Unified Global Events Marketing Strategy. Funded by the Treasury, this joint initiative boosted Cardano's brand awareness at 20 global tech conferences using a single, cohesive message. The campaign provided a promotional platform for 76 ecosystem projects, engaged over 7,500 participants (including a strong developer turnout), and initiated high-level institutional discussions with entities like the London Stock Exchange Group (LSEG).
