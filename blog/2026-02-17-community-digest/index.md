The February 17, 2026, Cardano Community Digest highlights the approved integration of LayerZero, which connects the network to over 150 blockchains and expands cross-chain liquidity. The report details the successful Cardano Africa Tech Summit in Nairobi and the release of node v10.5.4 featuring networking enhancements. Additionally, the Midnight mainnet is scheduled for late March, alongside the official launch of ADA futures on CME Group.
