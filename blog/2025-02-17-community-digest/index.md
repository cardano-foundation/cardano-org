Cardano Buidler Fest #2 is scheduled for April 24–25, 2025, in Da Nang, Vietnam. A key proposal by Andrew Westberg suggests reducing the treasury tax rate from 20% to 10% to adjust fund allocation. Additionally, Intersect MBO has released draft budget proposals for feedback, covering Research and Growth and Marketing strategies to encourage community input.
