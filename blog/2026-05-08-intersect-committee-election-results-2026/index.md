The April 2026 Intersect Committee Elections have concluded. A total of 102 candidates ran for 37 seats across eight committees, yielding a 24.8% voter turnout (187 members casting 3,967 ballots). Following an internal review and an independent audit by Dquadrant, the results were officially certified. Intersect also announced upcoming measures to strengthen member verification. The next elections are slated for 2027.
