The January 16, 2026, article by Laura Mattiucci addresses the declining confidence in financial reporting due to opaque data and outdated audit frameworks. It argues that organizations must shift to verifiable transparency using "trust-tech" architectures. The piece highlights Reeve, a Cardano Foundation product that anchors financial events to the Cardano blockchain for immutable evidence, and announces a new eBook detailing how this technology restores stakeholder trust.
