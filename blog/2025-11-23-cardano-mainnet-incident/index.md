Intersect published "Facts at a Glance" regarding the November 21 incident. A malformed transaction exploited a deserialization bug, causing a temporary chain partition while the network remained online and funds were safe. A coordinated response, including upgrades to node v10.5.2/v10.5.3, successfully restored full convergence within 14.5 hours.
